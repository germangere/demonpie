Nosotros

Demon Pie is a metal band from Mendoza City, Argentina. Its beginnings were in 2004, when Juan Pablo Mahia (vocals) and Matias San Martin (guitar) started the project as a New Metal Band. After recording his first album in 2005, Germán Geredus (drums) joined the band and they recorded a single in 2006. Over time the group was influenced by other styles within the metal genre (melodic death metal, metalcore, groove metal). which achieved an alternative style in their songs and a solid sound. The line-up remained intact until 2017, when Enzo Brunetti (bass) joined. In 2020 (Covid 19 pandemic), they recorded "The Arrival Live Streaming", which clearly shows the changes in the band's essence. They are currently producing their new plate at Havoc Studio, which aims to come out in mid-2022. For now they released their first single in December 2021: "Tu Victima", belonging to said album.
